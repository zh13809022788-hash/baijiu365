# Project Brief: Baijiu365

## Domain

baijiu365.com

## Working Name

Baijiu365

Editorial/product name options still usable in copy:

- Baijiu Field Guide
- The Baijiu Guide
- Baijiu Advocate
- China Spirits Guide

## One-Sentence Positioning

A practical, independent English-language guide to Chinese baijiu for overseas drinkers, hosts, bartenders, collectors, and China-curious travelers.

## Why This Exists

English-language baijiu content is thin, repetitive, or overly promotional. Many pages explain that baijiu is a Chinese spirit, but few help a reader understand what it tastes like, how to choose a bottle, how to drink it, how not to get fooled, and why it matters in real social settings.

Baijiu365 should become a useful guide, not a generic encyclopedia.

## Audience

Primary audience:

- Overseas spirits enthusiasts
- Bartenders and mixology readers
- Foreigners traveling to China
- Overseas Chinese gift buyers and hosts
- Importers, distributors, and restaurant operators researching baijiu

Secondary audience:

- Chinese readers who want English-language explanations of baijiu
- Producers or brands looking for international-facing education material

## Editorial Promise

Every page should help the reader make a better judgment about baijiu.

The site should avoid:

- Empty cultural slogans
- Generic AI encyclopedia prose
- Undisclosed sales pressure
- Overclaiming authority before real tasting data exists
- Pretending to be an association without contributors or evidence

The site should include:

- Practical buying/drinking guidance
- Clear flavor explanations
- Comparisons to familiar spirits
- Risks and tradeoffs
- Real-world context: banquets, gifts, restaurants, overseas buying, cocktails
- Transparent editorial standards

## MVP Scope

The first public version should include:

- Homepage
- Baijiu 101
- Types of Baijiu
- Best Baijiu for Beginners
- How to Drink Baijiu
- Baijiu vs Vodka
- Maotai Guide
- Glossary
- About / Editorial Standards

## Technical Direction

Use Astro static site generation.

Do not use Headless WordPress, databases, comments, login, or 10-language automation in Sprint 1.

Expected deployment target: Cloudflare Pages.
